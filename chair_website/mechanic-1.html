ai and scan 3d
Gom scan 1
- We use the GOM Scan 1 from Scantech3D Vietnam, which was created by the GOM cooperation. The GOM Scan 1 provides the data to AI, which calculates customers' statistics to make the best shape chair suitable.
ai and Chair
- AI learns the user's sitting attitude, automatically transforming the shape based on the customer's seat.

- The operation of AI in the chair will adjust to their body shape to ensure that their back is always covered by the chair back because people who work for an extended period tend to change their physical appearance and their sitting performance at different times.

- The unique things, the chair automatically adjusts its shape to match the activity being performed, such as typing, which necessitates a strange chair, reading, which requires a soft chair similar to a sofa, and gaming, among other things, because of flexible coupling into the chair.
- Moreover, our AI will provide visual assistance such as Google Assistant, Siri, and Alexa to play music on their chair.
